Our Work

We are raising money and awareness for Huntington’s Disease. Huntington’s Disease is an inherited genetic disorder that causes nerve cells in the brain to degenerate. The child of an affected parent will have a 50% chance of inheriting the disease due to the gene that causes it being dominant. This leads to a person losing mental and physical strength, causing problems with moving and thinking. Symptoms usually appear between ages 30-40 years. There is currently no known cure, so any money raised would go toward research to try and find one.
